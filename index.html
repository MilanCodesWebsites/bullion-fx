Users deposit funds directly into their BullionFX wallet for instant access to trading. The wallet supports real-time transfers and is tightly integrated with the platform’s trading engine.
Is BullionFX available internationally?
Yes, BullionFX is accessible to users globally, subject to regional regulations and compliance. We continuously expand our availability across compliant markets.
Which cryptocurrencies can I trade on BullionFX?
We support major coins like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and other trending assets. Our listings are regularly updated based on liquidity and demand.

Absolutely. BullionFX uses bank-grade encryption, biometric login options, two-factor authentication, and cold wallet storage for maximum protection.
